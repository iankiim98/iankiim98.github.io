About us

The ChickenRun fast food was established in 2009. By 2011, ChickenRun was located exclusively at its standalone outlets; Kahawa Sukari and Parklands, Nairobi. Since then, the number of outlets has increased to a total of 11 shops city wide. In the year 2018 ChickenRun grew its operations into Eldoret town, Uasin Gishu County, malls and business districts of major towns in Kenya. Additionally, we also operate a pizza sub franchise in all Tuskys supermarket nationwide. With this in mind, ChickenRun has risen in the fast-paced environment with the several outlets and will be a household name in terms of its products and services.

Market

We are dedicated to growing our brand and driving sales within all our partner restaurant sites through the use of a number of different ChickenRun marketing strategies. All these are analysed and monitored to ensure effectiveness to achieve the brand strategic objectives.
